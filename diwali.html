Diwali – Festival of Lights

Diwali is one of the most important festivals in India. It is known as the Festival of Lights. People decorate their homes with diyas, candles, and colorful lights. The festival symbolizes the victory of good over evil and light over darkness.

During Diwali, people clean and decorate their houses, make beautiful rangoli designs, and worship Goddess Lakshmi for wealth and prosperity. Families and friends exchange sweets and gifts and celebrate together with happiness and joy.

Fireworks and crackers are also an important part of Diwali celebrations. The entire country lights up with bright lamps, decorations, and festive spirit.
